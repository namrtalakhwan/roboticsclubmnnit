About The Club

We are a diverse group of robotics enthusiasts from all the departments of the college. The club is a student body which runs under the umbrella of Student Activity Centre MNNIT ALLAHABAD.
We build robots for academic purposes, to compete at national events or just out of freaky minds. We have a team of friendly experts equipped with all kind of tutorials and workshops along with a compelling workspace to make you an integral part of this expanding world....
